My Account/Profile

Why is my account banned?

If you find that you are receiving a notification that your account is banned, this means that you've either breached one of our Posting rules or there is a non-compliant activity as per our Trust and Safety rules relating to your account.

Business Users in case of Account banned are requested to contact their designated Account Manager
For any support feel free to Thrift Store Customer Services Team via the Contact form

Can other people see who I'm following?

Yes, they can. When someone comes across your profile, he or she can tap on your follower/following number and see who is following you and who you are following.
