Legal and privacy information

Why was my ad removed?

There are a few reasons why your ad may not appear immediately on our platform and could have possibly been removed. This depends on a few things like:

- You have violated our Rules

- You have posted a service or Items Not allowed

- Users flagged the ad as being spam or offensive

- The ad expired automatically after being listed for its full duration.

- Your ad has been reported to us and your access has been restricted

To ensure your ad does not get the boot, have a look at these most common reasons why your ad could have possibly been removed:

Posting duplicate ads are not cool and a definite no-go. If you post multiple items or even in multiple locations, your ads will be removed and could lead to your access being restricted.

We do not allow URLs /Emails/ Unverified Phone numbers in the title or description of your ad, however, you may add a URL in the description of your ad as long as it's a link to additional information or to a page with more pictures. We do not allow links that are designed solely to redirect traffic to another website.

Make sure the price of your item does not exceed or go drastically below the secondary market value.

If you post an offer for formal employment, we prefer you to use an active, legitimate business domain. You will be able to use your personal email address if you're posting an ad as a private individual looking to hire a nanny or domestic help.

Remember, your photos need to be relevant and appropriate. Bathroom or bedroom selfies are not nice!

Why isn't my ad active? It says, "pending".

Once you post an ad, you’ll receive an intimation right away to let you know the status of your ad. You can also have the option of checking the status of your ads in My Ads, which is located in the upper right hand corner.

Your ad will always be pending before it goes Live or in some cases get rejected. If it remains rejected, it's possibly because our filters have detected something which is against our rules..

These filters help keep our platform safe and duplicate free, by removing ads which have the same or similar ad titles and images - how cool is that? So, make sure that the title and images of your ads are not the same, and you should have no problems!

For other reasons, it would be a good idea to have another look at our Rules, and the list of the Prohibited Items and edit your Ad or where required post a new one.

For further assistance, you may call our helpline or connect to us via Live Chat.
